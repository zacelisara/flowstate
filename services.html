Our Services

Every business has a way of doing things.
We make sure it's a good one.

Three core services, each built to cut waste, save time, and put real money back in your pocket. No fluff, no overcomplicating things.

01
Discovery

Workflow Audit

Think of this as a health check for how your business actually runs. Not the way it looks on paper, the way it really works on a Monday morning.

We sit down with you and map out every step of your key processes. Where does information get stuck? Where are people doing the same work twice? Where's the time (and money) quietly disappearing?

You'll walk away with a clear, honest picture of what's working, what isn't, and exactly where the biggest opportunities are hiding.

What you get

  • Complete map of your current workflows
  • Clear list of bottlenecks and inefficiencies
  • Honest assessment of what's working and what isn't
  • Prioritised list of quick wins and bigger opportunities
  • A plain English report you can actually share with your team
02
Consulting

Process Design

Once we know where the problems are, we redesign your workflows from the ground up. Simpler. Cleaner. Easier for your team to follow without needing a 30 page manual.

We don't just hand you a document and walk away. We build your new processes with you, making sure your team is comfortable and confident before we step back. Because the best system in the world is useless if nobody uses it.

What you get

  • Redesigned workflows tailored to how your business actually operates
  • Clear step by step documentation your team can follow
  • Team onboarding and walkthrough sessions
  • Full ownership stays with you, no ongoing dependency on us
  • A process that scales as your team grows
03
Automation

Smart Automation

Not everything needs to be automated. But the things that do? We build those so they run on their own, saving your team real hours every single week.

We use tools like Airtable for managing your data in one place and n8n for connecting your systems so they talk to each other automatically. The result? Less copying and pasting, fewer mistakes, and your team free to focus on the work that actually needs a human brain.

What you get

  • Custom automations that match your exact workflows
  • Connected systems that share data automatically
  • Ongoing monitoring and support
  • Clear ROI tracking so you can see the payoff in real numbers
  • Documentation so your team understands what's happening behind the scenes

Common Questions

Things people usually ask

No silly questions here. If you're wondering it, someone else probably is too.

How much does it cost?

Every business is different, so we quote based on what you actually need. Start with a free consultation and we'll give you an honest picture. No surprises, no hidden fees.

Do I need to know anything about technology?

Not at all. We handle all the technical work. You just need to know your business, which you clearly already do. That's the important part.

How long does it take?

A typical audit takes about a week. Process redesign and automation builds vary depending on complexity, but we always give you a clear timeline upfront so there are no surprises.

Can you work with our existing tools?

Absolutely. We're vendor neutral, meaning we work with whatever you already have. If something better exists for your situation, we'll let you know, but the choice is always yours.

What if we don't have any systems at all?

Even better. Starting fresh means we can set you up right from the beginning, without having to undo bad habits. We'll build something that fits the way you work.

Are you just one person?

Yes, and that's a feature, not a bug. You get direct access to the person doing the work. No account managers, no being passed around. Just one consistent point of contact who knows your business inside and out.
