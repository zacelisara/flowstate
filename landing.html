Common questions

Things people ask before they book.

Are you really just one person?
Yes — and that's a feature, not a bug. You talk directly to the person doing the work. No account managers, no being passed around. One consistent point of contact who knows your business inside out.
What if automation isn't the right answer for us?
Then I'll say so on the free call. Sometimes the right fix is a better spreadsheet and clearer process — no custom builds needed. My reputation matters more than a quick sale.
Do we need specific software?
No. We're vendor neutral. We'll work with Airtable, n8n, Notion, HubSpot, Xero, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365 — whatever you already have. If something better exists for your case, we'll flag it, but the choice is yours.
How long does it take to see results?
Audits wrap inside a week. Most Design & Build engagements have their first automation live within 3–4 weeks. Clients usually recover the cost of the engagement in the first quarter.
What happens if you get hit by a bus?
Everything we build is documented, owned by you, and runs on platforms you control. If I disappeared tomorrow, your workflows keep running and any competent operator could pick up the docs. That's the standard.
